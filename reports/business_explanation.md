# Business Explanation

## revenue_forecast_next_30_days.csv

Answers: What revenue should the business expect each day over the next 30 days?

Use this file for short-term revenue planning and daily sales expectations.

## profit_forecast_next_30_days.csv

Answers: What gross profit should the business expect each day over the next 30 days?

Use this file to understand near-term profitability, not just sales volume.

## product_store_demand_forecast.csv

Answers: How many units of each product are expected to sell at each store over the next 7 and 30 days?

Use this file as the demand input for restock planning.

## restock_recommendations_next_7_days.csv

Answers: Which products are likely to need restocking, where, and how urgent are they?

Use this file to prioritize store and product replenishment based on forecast demand, current stock, safety stock, and margin.

## top_profit_products_next_30_days.csv

Answers: Which products are expected to generate the most gross profit over the next 30 days?

Use this file to focus merchandising, promotion, and inventory attention on products with the strongest expected profit contribution.
